Song Joong Ki

Biography

Song Joong Ki is a South Korean actor born on September 19, 1985 in Daejeon, South Korea. Growing up, he was a shy straight A student. is parrents enrolled him in extracurricular classes to help him conquer his shyness and improve his social skills. One of those classes was acting, where he slowly developed a passsion of the art. Although he was enrolled in the business major during college, he eventually left his studies to persue acting.

Joonki rose to fame with the drama Sungkyunkwan Scandal in (2010) and with the variety appearance in the television show Running Man as one of the oroginal cast members in 2010. Since then, he has starred in many dramas and films. He has also won many film awards since his debut in 2008.

Before his two-years mandatory military service in 2013, he held a fan meeting to temporary say good bye to his fans. Joong Ki was discharged on May 26, 2015 and had a comeback to his acting career with the drama drama Descendants of the Sun, which is currently airing on television.
